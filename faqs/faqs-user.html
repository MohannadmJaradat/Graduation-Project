This Conference Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the process of organizing, managing, and participating in conferences. It includes features for submission management, review processes, scheduling, attendee management, and more.

To create an account, click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

To submit a paper or abstract, log in to your account, go to the desired conference, and follow the instructions to upload your document and provide necessary details.

In order to be a reviewer, you must be invited by the manager or the supervisor of the conference.

To register for a conference, log in to your account, navigate to the 'Conferences' section, select the conference you want to attend, and click on the 'Join' button. Fill out the registration form and complete the payment process.

Our system is compatible with the latest versions of major browsers such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. For the best experience, ensure your browser is up to date.

To update your profile information, log in to your account, go to the 'Profile' section, and make the necessary changes. Don't forget to save your changes before exiting the page.

You can contact support by clicking on the "Contact" link in the navigation menu and filling out the support form.
